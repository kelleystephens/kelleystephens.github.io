Hi! I'm Kelley! I am a full-stack software developer in the Nashville area.

In 2014, I realized I needed a career change so I quit my job in operations & joined the Nashville Software School. After 6 months of an intensive, hands-on development bootcamp, I entered into my first position as a Software Engineer at Ingram Content Group. For the next three years, I learned, grew, & deepened my passion for software development.

In 2017, I was contacted for a new opportunity at Ramsey Solutions. I joined the development team as the first female developer in the company. There I was able to be mentored, mentor others, give lightning talks, develop, QA, debug, and much more.

After having two beautiful babies, I've decided it's time to branch out own my own for increased flexibility & opportunities. I'm excited to work with a diverisfied client base in order to design and build elegant solutions for many different project types.

In my spare time, I love visiting local Nashville spots with my family- restaurants, maker's markets, parks, & local boutique shops. I also love spending time at home with my hubby, the kiddos, our fun-loving pup & lazy ragdoll cat. I spend my Sundays at Church of the City in Franklin and my weekdays wearing the multiple hats of a developer, mom, wife, & friend. To say we are busy is an understatement, but we wouldn't have it any other way.
